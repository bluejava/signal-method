# Product Goals

Purpose
Capture the current big-picture product direction that should guide roadmap and feature planning.

Include

- target users or stakeholders
- desired outcomes
- current priorities
- non-goals
- success signals
- constraints that shape planning

Exclude

- phase-by-phase sequencing
- feature implementation detail
- historical rationale for past decisions
- active workflow progress

Split when
Multiple product areas have independent planning cycles, audiences, or success criteria.

## Current Direction

Describe the product direction that should shape the next planning horizon.

## Target Users

List the users, operators, or stakeholders whose outcomes matter most.

## Desired Outcomes

Describe the outcomes the product should make possible.

## Priorities

List the current priorities in order.

## Non-Goals

List goals that are intentionally outside the current planning horizon.

## Success Signals

Describe the qualitative or quantitative signals that would show progress.

## Constraints

List constraints that should shape roadmap and feature decisions.
